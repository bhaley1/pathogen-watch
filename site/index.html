Every morning, Pathogen Watch reads the latest NCBI Pathogen Detection snapshot and surfaces SNP-based clusters that mix human clinical cases with isolates from food, animals, or the environment. New clusters and new additions to existing clusters are tracked day-over-day; nonhuman isolates within tight SNP distance of others are flagged as early-warning candidates.

About

Pathogen Watch is an open-access daily surveillance digest of human–animal–environmental SNP clusters in foodborne pathogens, derived from the NCBI Pathogen Detection nightly snapshot. Built by Bradd Haley, PhD at the USDA Agricultural Research Service.

Source code: github.com/bhaley1/pathogen-watch
